# Code of Conduct

Perspecta aims to maintain a respectful and professional community.
By participating in this project, you agree to engage in a way
that supports constructive collaboration and treats other people with care.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and courteous in discussions and reviews.
- Give constructive feedback and assume good intent.
- Collaborate in good faith and stay focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Abusive, threatening, or intimidating language or behavior.
- Sharing someone else's private information without permission.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
at tim@cogan.dev.

## Enforcement

Reports will be reviewed and reasonable action may be taken to protect the
community and keep collaboration productive.
